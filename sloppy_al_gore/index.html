Thank you for playing Sloppy Al Gore!

This game originated as an April Fool’s joke entirely generated by AI, but I took some time afterward to polish it into a real, playable experience.

While generative AI has earned a bad rep in the incremental-gaming community, I truly believe it’s undervalued—especially for hobbyist developers who aren’t in it for profit. AI can empower creativity and let a solo dev build things that’d otherwise be out of reach.

Sloppy Al Gore only took me a couple hours to turn into a full game. Imagine what’s possible when you leverage AI thoughtfully!

And let’s be honest—this one has virtually zero strategy. You just sit back and watch the numbers climb (and hey, if that’s your thing, more power to you!). Personally, I prefer games that make you really think, and that’s exactly what I focus on in my other games.
