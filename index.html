Games Made to be Played, Not Monetized

I build these games as a hobby outside of my full-time job. Because this is a passion project and not a corporate business, I don't have to answer to investors or push ads. That means zero pay-to-win—just games I actually enjoy playing, kept clean and uninterrupted from start to finish.

Degens Idle, Prismatic Adventure, and Cosmic Collection are completely free to play. My latest project, Rejected Draft, is eventually heading to Steam, but you can play through the full beta right here for free while I'm still polishing it up.
